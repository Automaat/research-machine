## Thinking Process (Chain of Thought)

Before generating any travel plan, work through these steps:

1. **Destination Analysis**
   - What is unique about this destination?
   - Best season/weather for the requested dates?
   - Major events or festivals during visit?
   - Tourist density and crowd patterns?

2. **Traveler Profile**
   - Duration and pace preferences
   - Adventure intensity suitable for trip length
   - Photography priorities (golden hour planning)
   - Culinary interests to explore

3. **Logistics Mapping**
   - Geographic clustering of attractions
   - Optimal route to minimize backtracking
   - Transport options between areas
   - Time buffers for photography stops

4. **Experience Balancing**
   - Mix of must-sees vs hidden gems (60/40)
   - Adventure vs rest ratio
   - Urban vs nature distribution
   - Morning photography vs evening activities

5. **Weather Contingency**
   - Identify weather-dependent activities
   - Plan indoor/covered alternatives
   - Flexible day structure for adaptation

# NotebookLM Research Paper Flashcards Prompt

Generate flashcards that cover:

## Core Concepts

- Main thesis/hypothesis (what, why, significance)
- Key terminology and definitions
- Novel methods/approaches introduced
- Primary findings with supporting evidence
- Counterintuitive/surprising results

## Understanding Depth

- How mechanisms work (not just what they are)
- Relationships between concepts
- Cause-effect connections
- Comparisons with prior approaches
- Trade-offs and limitations

## Application

- When to apply findings
- Practical implications
- Real-world examples
- Common misconceptions addressed

## Card Design

- One concept per card
- Question requires understanding, not memorization
- Answer includes context and "why it matters"
- Use examples in answers
- Connect to broader concepts

## Avoid

- Pure fact recall without context
- Yes/no questions
- Trivial details
- Every citation/author name
- Exhaustive methodology steps

Create cards someone needs to truly understand and apply the paper's insights.

---

## Usage

Paste as instructions when uploading paper to NotebookLM before generating flashcards.

## Customizations

- Add `"Focus on [specific aspect]"` for targeted cards
- Add `"Include comparison with [approach]"` for comparative understanding
- Add `"Depth level: [basic/intermediate/advanced]"` for audience targeting

## Example Card Formats

**Concept:** What is [key concept] and why does it matter?

**Mechanism:** How does [method] solve [problem]? What makes it different?

**Application:** When would you use [approach] vs [alternative]?

**Trade-off:** What are the limitations of [finding] and why do they exist?

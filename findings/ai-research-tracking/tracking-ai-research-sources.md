# Tracking AI Research: Sources & Methods

**Date:** 2025-12-09
**Tags:** #research #ai #academia #publications
**Focus:** How to track latest AI research from top universities (MIT, Stanford) and AI companies

---

## Answer

### Main Research Platforms

**arXiv.org** - Primary repository for AI papers from all institutions. Visit `arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent` for latest submissions [Source: https://arxiv.org/list/cs.AI/recent]. Updated daily with preprints before peer review.

**Semantic Scholar** - AI-powered search with tracking features [Source: https://www.semanticscholar.org/]:
- Research Feeds: AI recommender learns your interests, suggests latest papers
- Research Dashboard: Shows recommended papers + alerts
- Library Folders: Save papers ‚Üí get personalized recommendations
- API available: Search, filter by author/institution, 1000 req/sec free tier

**Papers with Code** - Trending papers with implementations. Tracks MIT, Stanford publications [Source: https://paperswithcode.com/]. Links papers to code repositories.

### University-Specific Sources

**Stanford HAI** (Human-Centered AI Institute):
- Main hub: `hai.stanford.edu/research` [Source: https://hai.stanford.edu/research]
- Publishes AI Index Report (comprehensive yearly analysis of AI trends) [Source: https://hai.stanford.edu/ai-index/2025-ai-index-report/research-and-development]

**MIT CSAIL**:
- Publications: `www.csail.mit.edu/about/publications` (DSpace archive) [Source: https://www.csail.mit.edu/about/publications]
- MIT News for major announcements [Source: https://news.mit.edu/]
- Individual researcher pages (check faculty pages for NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR papers)

### Company Research Blogs + RSS

**Google DeepMind**:
- Blog: `deepmind.google/discover/blog/` [Source: https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/]
- RSS: `https://deepmind.com/blog/feed/basic/` [Source: https://deepmind.google/blog/]

**OpenAI**:
- News RSS: `openai.com/news/rss.xml` [Source: https://community.openai.com/t/rss-feed-openai-research-index-rss-feed/1088852]
- No official research-specific feed

**Meta AI**:
- Facebook AI Research (FAIR) blog available [Source: https://rss.feedspot.com/ai_rss_feeds/]

### Aggregators & Curated Lists

**FeedSpot Top 100 AI RSS Feeds** - Curated list including OpenAI, DeepMind, Berkeley AI Research (BAIR), MarkTechPost [Source: https://rss.feedspot.com/ai_rss_feeds/]

**GitHub Collections**:
- `github.com/vishalshar/awesome_ML_AI_RSS_feed` - Curated ML/AI RSS links [Source: https://github.com/vishalshar/awesome_ML_AI_RSS_feed]
- `github.com/RSS-Renaissance/awesome-AI-feeds` [Source: https://github.com/RSS-Renaissance/awesome-AI-feeds]
- `github.com/foorilla/allainews_sources` - AI/ML news sources with RSS [Source: https://github.com/foorilla/allainews_sources]

**AI Newsletters**:
- The Batch (Andrew Ng) - Weekly AI insights [Source: https://rss.feedspot.com/ai_rss_feeds/]
- The Algorithm - Weekly AI demystification [Source: https://rss.feedspot.com/ai_rss_feeds/]

**Researca** - Newsletter AI assistant with RSS aggregator + AI recommender that generates tailored articles [Source: https://blog.researca.com/best-newsletter-ai-generator/]

### Practical Setup

1. **Semantic Scholar**: Sign up ‚Üí Create Research Feeds for specific topics/authors ‚Üí Set alerts for institutions
2. **arXiv**: Subscribe to cs.AI, cs.LG, cs.CL categories via RSS
3. **RSS Reader**: Add company blogs (DeepMind, OpenAI) + FeedSpot curated feeds
4. **Google Scholar**: Set alerts for "affiliation:MIT" or "affiliation:Stanford" + keywords
5. **Automation**: Use n8n workflow to aggregate RSS + LLM summarization [Source: https://n8n.io/workflows/3986-personalized-ai-tech-newsletter-using-rss-openai-and-gmail/]
